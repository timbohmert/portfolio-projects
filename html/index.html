about me: I am Timmy B.

In Lakeville, Minnesota,born and raised.
Dreaming of medical school is how I spent most of my days.
Studying the MCAT, taxing it out, not cool.
And working in healthcare outside of school.
When a couple of my guys thought it would be good,
started making a finance app best we could.
I got one little taste and for coding I cared.
And said "I'm moving with a career in development of software."

About two years ago, two of my colleagues and myself started ideating around a stock market competition game. In the development of our app, Socap, I became intrigued with building the user experience and user interface. Once I got to a level of making the GUI on Adobe XD, I became increasingly curious about the machine that would create functionality in such an app. This became the impetus for a growing interest in computer science. it was then in September 2019 that I decided to change course from pursuing medical school and go all in on learning programming.

the beginnings

Candide was born in the Alps of France on the 22nd of May 1982. In his youth, Candide showed talent as a mogul skier. Yet, he was quickly drawn to the freeform nature of freestyle snowboarding, and he started to experiment on bringing some of those traits to freestyle skiing.
